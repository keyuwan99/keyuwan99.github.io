Hi! My name is Keyu Wan. I am a fourth-year undergrad at Caltech， majoring in Mechanical Engineering specializing in Robotics, and Control and Dynamic Systems (CDS). I am fortunate to work with Prof. Aaron Ames, Prof. Chiara Daraio, Prof. Mory Gharib, and Prof. Yuki Oka from Caltech, and Prof. Shriya Srinivasan from Harvard.

My goal is to enable assistive robotics that restore human function and enhance mobility through the integration of physiological sensing, biomechanics, and intelligent control within a closed-loop framework.

You are welcomed to take a look at my CV here.
